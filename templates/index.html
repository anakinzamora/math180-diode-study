Semiconductor Diode Leakage Current Study
MATH180 · Factorial Design · Mapua University
Two-Way ANOVA

About this study

This simulation models leakage current (I₀) in a silicon p-n junction diode using the Shockley diode equation: I₀ = A·T²·e−Eg/kT. Two factors are varied: doping concentration and junction temperature. A two-way factorial ANOVA determines which factor significantly affects leakage current.

Experiment parameters

  • Factor A: Doping Concentration — Low (1×10¹⁵), Medium (1×10¹⁷), High (1×10¹⁹) cm⁻³
  • Factor B: Temperature — 27°C, 50°C, 75°C
  • Design: 3×3 factorial · 5 replicates · 45 trials
  • Significance level: α = 0.05
